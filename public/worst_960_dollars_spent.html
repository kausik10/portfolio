The worst $960 I ever spent; ASUS TUF F15

“As a Computer Engineering student, laptop is the most essential device.”

Back me off here guys! I know everyone would agree with me here.

On December 2021, I took one of the most important decisions for upcoming 5 years. I bought a laptop for pursuing my Bachelor’s in Computer Engineering. I searched the internet for the best laptop suitable for my price range and capable of doing things that I wanted during my ‘university era’.

My budget: $970

I chose: ASUS TUF F15 2021 model

Why? It was slim, sleek and the display was amazing!

Let me list out the specs for you.

These were some of the major highlights of the laptop I had bought.

With a Backlit keyboard, a 6000 DPI mouse, I was on heaven playing games the first few weeks. I was happy with my laptop. Until something went terribly wrong!

What went Wrong?

6 months in, my laptop started freezing abruptly. At first, the audio was distorted (like a disturbed radio message on sci-fi films). Then the wifi disappeared. And I could do nothing. My only option was to force shutdown the laptop and reboot again.
